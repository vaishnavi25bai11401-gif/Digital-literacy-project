## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project completed as part of the CSE0001 course. In this project, I take on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador whose responsibility is to promote digital awareness among fellow students.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate practical knowledge of digital tools, professional online presence, digital communication, and cyber safety. The project consists of five tasks that explore different aspects of digital literacy. All the work, screenshots, and documents related to these tasks are organized in this repository.

## Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. The design highlights key topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and maintaining a professional online presence.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created or updated professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help students showcase their skills, projects, and achievements while building a strong digital presence.
