# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students

## Do's
- Think before posting and ensure your content reflects positively on you.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account privacy settings.
- Share accurate and verified information only.
- Maintain respectful communication with others online.
- Use social media for learning, networking, and professional growth.

## Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not spread rumors or unverified news.
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying.
- Do not spend excessive time on social media that affects studies or productivity.
