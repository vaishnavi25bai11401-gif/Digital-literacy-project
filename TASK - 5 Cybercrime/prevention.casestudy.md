# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

## Online Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Never click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages.
- Avoid sharing personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or ID details publicly.
- Always verify unknown calls or messages claiming to be from banks, delivery services, or government officials.
- Keep your phone, apps, and operating system updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on public networks.

## Financial / UPI Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve payment requests unless you are sure you are sending money intentionally.
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before confirming any UPI transaction.

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime:
- Report immediately on the **National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in**
- Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** to report financial fraud quickly.
